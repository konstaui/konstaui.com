## NavbarBackLink Props

`NavbarBackLink` should be placed in Navbar's "left" area:

```jsx
<Navbar title="My App">
  {#snippet left()}
  <NavbarBackLink text="Back" onClick={() => history.back()} />
  {/snippet}
</Navbar>
```
